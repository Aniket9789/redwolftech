OUR JOURNEY

RedWolf Hi-T ech embarked on its journey in 2018 with a bold vision to disrupt the tech industry. Led by Rohit Jain, Chirag Sachdeva, and Pankaj Chaturvedi, the company quickly gained recognition for its innovative approach and commitment to excellence. In the same year, we introduced our flagship product, Finance Locker, marking the beginning of a transformative era in financial management solutions.

FINANCE LOCKER

Finance Locker, our pioneering product launched in 2018, redefined the way retailers manage their finances. With models such as T-Cops Zt Pro, T-Cops Zt Plus, and T-Cops I Mac, Finance Locker empowered retailers to efficiently oversee their financed customers and streamline the payment process. In the event of delayed EMI payments, T-Cops I Mac provided a seamless solution, ensuring smooth transactions and enhanced customer satisfaction.

REDWOLF EV

In 2024, RedWolf Hi-Tech expanded its portfolio with the launch of RedWolf EV, an electric vehicle aimed at revolutionizing urban mobility. With a focus on sustainability and innovation, RedWolf EV offers eco-friendly transportation solutions that are both efficient and reliable. Designed for urban commuters, RedWolf EV scooters combine cutting-edge technology with sleek design, offering a smooth and enjoyable riding experience. With RedWolf EV, we are committed to reducing carbon emissions and building a greener future for generations to come.
